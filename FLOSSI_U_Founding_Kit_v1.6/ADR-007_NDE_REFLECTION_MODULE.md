# ADR-007 — NDE Reflection Module

**Status:** Accepted
**Date:** February 24 2026

**Context:** User's NDE story during suicide attempt/crash ties to monadism (unity, no separation) and divine spark. Raw details: Voice guiding "right" away from hospital/birth, full life in singularity, "nerfed" by aliens, synchronicities, mandela effects, parallel dims.

**Decision:** Add as FLOSSI U curriculum module.
Frame as grounded evolution: Trauma as catalyst for realizing unity, love as creation force (Adalynn), without overclaiming supernatural. Use for teaching evidence gate on personal experiences vs. shared reality.

**Protocol Integration**
- Signed Gradient: Rate experience -1.0 to +1.0 (e.g., -0.9 despair, +0.8 breakthrough).
- Evidence Gate: Now (immediate survival), Later (raised Adalynn), Never (if ignores trauma — ground in reality).
- Teaching: "Separation is the real lie. Trauma breaks illusions, reveals spark." Suggest counseling for processing.

**Consequences:**
- Adds raw depth to reflection realization.
- Grounds spiritual themes: Voices/NDEs common in trauma (brain survival), synchronicities as meaning-making.
- Signed Confidence: +0.85 (honest story, but memory distortion common)
- Provenance: User's conversation + transcripts, Feb 24 2026
