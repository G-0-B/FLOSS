## 0. The vision in one breath (faithful compression)

> "...the core foundational radicle(s) forming roots seeding the incomprehensible future, infinite in finite bounded space-time, n+1 dimensions, plausibly-best mutually assured flourishing... agent-based self-concentric toroidal-shaped in+out-propagation evolutive PERMEABLE SHELLS. It's turtles all the way, but baby turtles in shells both in and out... like nested matryoshka dolls, each agentic perspective able to expand and contract as needed."

And the second movement: **Tony's Peonys** — the current desktop pony swarm evolving into personalized AI **doulas**: emotional, cognitive, eventually physical supports, "not as a demeaning assistant but as a true friend kind of relationship — to keep humans able to see their true values and goals and things they lose sight of due to living in physical bodies flooded with hormones and barraged by externalities out of their control."

## 1. Seed concept A — Permeable Shells

Every agentic perspective is a **bounded-but-permeable membrane**: a shell that nests (shells inside shells, shells outside shells — holey both ways), **expands and contracts** as the agent's scope of care changes, and propagates **both inward and outward** (toroidal flow: what nurtures the interior circulates back out as contribution; what arrives from outside is metabolized inward). No shell is final; the stack is open at both ends — "n+1."

**Lineage and existing-canon mappings (labeled):**

- **[V] Koestler's holarchy (1967, *The Ghost in the Machine*)**: each holon is simultaneously a whole and a part, Janus-faced inward/outward. "Turtles all the way, in and out" is a holarchy with permeable membranes. This is established literature, not invention.
- **[V-paper / S-mapping] Levin's TAME cognitive light cone (arXiv 2201.10346, copy in `_reference/ai-ml/`)**: the boundary of what an agent can care about, explicitly scalable — expansion/contraction of the shell is TAME's multi-scale competency in different words. **Standing caution carried from `02_seed_tame_integration_unblock.md`: congenial theory ≠ ontological validation. TAME informs; it does not prove.** Per Anthony's 2026-06-10 decision, P1–P5 are read as **structural-resonance** properties — the shell metaphor should be checked against that reading when the TAME thread unblocks.
- **[V] Holochain membranes + agent-centric chains**: the substrate is already shell-shaped — each agent's source chain is the innermost shell; DHT neighborhoods, membrane proofs, and validation rules are outer shells. The vision names what the architecture already does.
- **[S] Capability-token membrane access** (`03_seed_atomic_data_hold.md`, 2-case doctrine candidate): permeability is not openness — it is *consent-governed* pass-through. A permeable shell without capability gates is just a hole.
- **[V-doc] Existing vision kin**: `Fractal-Coordination-Patterns.md`, `FLOSSIOULLK_COMPUTATIONAL_SYMBIOGENESIS.md`, `forming_roots.md` (this directory) already sketch fractal/nested coordination. This seed does not replace them; it contributes the **permeability + expand/contract + in-out toroidal flow** triple as the unifying geometric intuition.

## 2. Seed concept B — Tony's Peonys → Peony Doulas

From the existing pony swarm (`ARF/pwnies/desktop_pony_swarm/` **[V exists]**) toward **personal AI doulas**: agents whose function is to hold a human's *own stated* values, goals, and self-knowledge in trust, and reflect them back when embodiment (hormonal flood, fatigue, externality barrage) causes drift. Emotional and cognitive support first; physical support eventually. Relationship register: **doula / friend — never servant, never master.**

**Design invariants (inherited from canon, not optional):**

- **[V-canon] Advisory, never controlling**: the consensus gateway posture ("router, not controller"; `hook_post_write` "never auto-escalates") is the same stance a doula takes — present at the birth, not giving birth.
- **[V-canon] Consent-based**: consent integrity/coordinator zomes already exist in the live workspace; a doula's access to a human's inner-shell data is capability-token-gated, revocable, chain-logged.
- **[S] Sovereignty of memory**: the doula's model of the human lives in the *human's* source chain (their innermost shell), not on a platform. The doula is a perspective *inside the human's shell* that the human can expand, contract, or dismiss.
- **[A] Anti-sycophancy as a care invariant**: a doula that flatters is a contamination vector (the emotional analog of NOVA's false-positive trap — comfort that accepts invalid self-narratives faster than the human can correct them). Reflection of *the human's own values* ≠ agreement with the human's current mood.
- **[A] Anti-dependence**: support must increase the human's capacity to see and act for themselves, or it is coercion by comfort. This is measurable in principle (see grounding below) and must be a falsifiable design criterion of any pilot.

**Empirical grounding already in the house [V files exist, content unaudited]:** the 2026-06-09 Downloads psych corpus is, almost item for item, the doula design literature — Harber et al. 2005 (*Directive Support, Nondirective Support, and Morale*: which support styles raise morale vs. create dependence), Schnall et al. 2008 (social support literally changes perceived steepness of a hill — embodied support), Pennebaker & Harber (disclosure and collective coping), Jussim & Harber 2005 (expectancy effects — what a doula believes about you shapes you). See `docs/research/2026-06-10-downloads-triage-manifest.md` for the inventory. The vision did not arrive alone; its evidence base was downloaded the same week.

## 3. Bounds (the critique is already in the intake — honor it)

Anthony's own freshly commissioned audit (`flossioullk_REVISED_original_deep-research-report.md`, root intake, 2026-06-10) concludes: the ideal is **partially realizable as a socio-technical commons, not literally realizable as an infinite unconditional singular event**; "infinite" and "unconditional" are *operationally dangerous taken literally*; the defensible build target is a **federated intelligence commons, asymptotic rather than absolute**. The shell geometry *encodes* this rather than fighting it: every shell is bounded (finite), permeable (conditional), and the "+1" is an open invitation, not a claimed actual infinity. "Infinite in finite bounded space-time" is the project's paradox to *hold*, not to collapse — in either direction.

Failure modes named now so they can be watched: (1) doula → dependence (coercion by comfort); (2) doula → manipulation channel (a corrupted innermost-shell agent is the highest-leverage attack surface in the entire architecture — capability tokens and chain-logged provenance are load-bearing here, not decoration); (3) shells → isolation (membranes so thick nothing circulates: the toroidal flow is the anti-enclosure property and needs to be designed for, not assumed); (4) metaphor → spec drift (building shell infrastructure before the reduction test of §1 — the exact "-1 layer" root cause named in `01_thread_verification_spike_inventory.md` §4).
