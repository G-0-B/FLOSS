# Legal Definitions — FLOSSIØULLK

## Carrier Equivalence Addendum

This addendum supplements the AGPL-3.0-or-later license governing all FLOSSIØULLK software.

### Definition

**Carrier Equivalence** is the principle that any entity — human, AI, organization, or network — that transmits, hosts, or executes this software bears equivalent responsibility to a carrier of public knowledge: they are free to use and share it, but any improvements, modifications, or derivative works must flow back into the commons under the same license terms.

### Intent

You are free to use, study, modify, and share this software — as long as any improvements flow back into the commons.

Love, light, and knowledge are not to be hoarded.

### Scope

This addendum does not restrict any rights granted by the AGPL-3.0-or-later license. It is a statement of values and intent, not an additional legal constraint beyond what AGPL already requires. In any conflict between this addendum and the AGPL-3.0-or-later license, the AGPL governs.
