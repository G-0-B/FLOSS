At the second level, a metameme is the pattern that governs how memes spread, mutate, resist infection, or get selected, which is exactly the kind of concern raised in the memetics text’s focus on mind viruses, selection pressures, and deliberate reprogramming.[^1_2]

Your phrase adds a third level: the **meta** is not just a story about ideas, but an environment that can modify its own selection conditions through recursive improvement, explicit provenance, continuation vectors, and machine-ingestible structure.
