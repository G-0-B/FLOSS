## II. The P+K Framework: Formalizing Scale-Free Cognition

The epistemological foundation of the integration is the \(P+K\) framework, drawn directly from Chis-Ciure and Levin's 2025 *Synthese* paper *Cognition All the Way Down 2.0*.[^3][^5][^1]

### The Problem Space Quintuple

A problem space is defined as a quintuple:

\[ P = \langle S, O, C, E, H \rangle \]

where:
- \(S\) = discrete definable states
- \(O\) = available operators or transition mechanisms
- \(C\) = active environmental and systemic constraints
- \(E\) = evaluation metrics determining progress
- \(H\) = temporal or spatial horizon limiting scope

This formalism is *substrate-neutral*: it applies equally to a bacterium navigating a glucose gradient, a planarian flatworm regenerating its morphology, a Holochain zome resolving a validation conflict, and a large language model navigating a semantic embedding space.[^1][^3]

### The Search Efficiency Metric K

The magnitude of intelligence—designated the *search efficiency metric* \(K\)—is quantified as:

\[ K = \log_{10}\!\left(\frac{\tau_{\text{blind}}}{\tau_{\text{agent}}}\right) \]

This logarithmic ratio measures how many orders of magnitude of dissipative work an agential policy saves relative to a maximal-entropy (random walk) search strategy. Empirically calibrated benchmarks from the Levin corpus include:[^3]

| Biological System | Problem Space | Approximate K Value | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|---|
| *Dictyostelium discoideum* (chemotaxis) | Glucose gradient navigation | ≈ 2.18–2.30 | ~150–200× more efficient than random walk |
| Planarian head regeneration (BaCl₂ disruption) | Morphogenetic space recovery | ≈ 21 | 10²¹× more efficient than random molecular assembly |
| Human spatial navigation (hippocampus) | 3D behavioral space | High; manifold-encoded | Representational homeomorphism to physical environment |

The \(K\)-metric provides a *lineage-traceable benchmark* that scales seamlessly across phylogenetic and technological disparities. By applying this metric to FLOSSI0ULLK agents, multi-agent federations can be quantitatively evaluated on their \(K\)-ledger efficiency when navigating the informational, logical, and semantic spaces of the decentralized web.[^1][^3]

### Nested Markov Blankets and Additive Composability

Crucially, nested Markov blankets—the statistical boundaries separating a system's internal states from its external environment—ensure that \(K\) is *additively compositional*. When systems merge or couple voluntarily, their combined intelligence compounds. This is the mathematical basis for the FLOSSI0ULLK principle that evolutionary and architectural transitions generate massive expansions in competency rather than averaging down.[^3]

### Constraints as Enablements

A non-obvious corollary of the \(P+K\) formalism is that *relaxing or editing constraints* \(C\) within a given problem space more frequently drives problem-space retiling than introducing new operators \(O\). Biological regulation operates less like a centralized gate and more like a distributed network of *affordance amplifiers*. Dopamine, for instance, modulates the refresh rate of abstract problem-space retiling within the mammalian mesocortical tract, functionally amplifying the system's capacity to perceive alternative solutions. For FLOSSI0ULLK, this dictates that system governance should prioritize *what agents choose not to do*—designing the architecture around constraints as enablements allows discovery of highly optimized search strategies without triggering centralized computational overhead.[^1][^3]
