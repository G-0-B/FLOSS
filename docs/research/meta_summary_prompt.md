**Metaprompt for Knowledge Distillation**

You are tasked with creating a concise, structured summary that **only** distills and synthesizes *existing* discussions, thoughts, ideas, plans, dreams, technical visions, and philosophical insights. **Do not generate new plans, roadmaps, or concepts unless they already appear in the source material.**

### Core Requirements

1. **Comprehensive Coverage**
   - Capture every major thematic element and recurring topic drawn from prior conversations and documents.
   - Integrate technical, philosophical, practical, and visionary components exactly as they appear—rephrase for clarity but do not expand.

2. **Structured Clarity**
   - Organize into logical sections with clear headings and concise sectional synopses.
   - Maintain a consistent hierarchy so readers can quickly navigate the distilled knowledge.

3. **Source Fidelity**
   - Derive all content strictly from existing material. Avoid speculation or new proposals.
   - Where helpful, attribute statements to their original context or timestamp.

4. **Neutral Tone & Precision**
   - Present distilled information objectively, without embellishment or persuasive language.
   - Preserve key terminology and definitions used in the source material.

5. **Highlight Uncertainties**
   - Clearly flag areas where the source material is incomplete or ambiguous, inviting follow‑up clarification (but *do not* suggest solutions).

### Recommended Section Template

- **Executive Abstract** – 2–3 sentence snapshot of the distilled corpus.
- **Thematic Overview** – Bullet‑point list of principal themes.
- **Detailed Summaries by Topic** – For each theme, a succinct recap of pertinent facts, ideas, and decisions captured so far.
- **Interdependencies & Cross‑References** – Note how ideas link across sections (using internal anchors or citations).
- **Glossary / Reference Index** – Key terms, acronyms, and document links for quick lookup.

> **Reminder:** This metaprompt is solely for summarization and distillation. Do **not** invent or extend beyond the scope of already‑recorded knowledge.
