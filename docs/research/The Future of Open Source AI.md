## Foundational Infrastructure Projects

### Holochain: Agent-Centric Distributed Computing

Holochain represents a paradigm shift from blockchain-based systems to agent-centric architecture. The **Holochain Foundation** has recently restructured in 2025[1][2], spinning off **Unyt, Inc.** to further develop mutual-credit accounting systems[1]. Key developments include:

- **Holochain 0.5.3** released July 2025 with networking fixes and enhancements[3]
- **Holochain 0.6** currently 48% complete with 6 months remaining[3]
- Framework enabling peer-to-peer applications without servers or blockchains[3]
- Integration with distributed databases and consensus-free validation[3]

The platform's **agent-centric architecture** allows each participant to maintain their own chain while enabling cryptographic validation across the network[3]. This creates resilient, scalable systems that don't rely on global consensus mechanisms.

### Distributed Vector Databases for AI

The explosion of AI applications has driven innovation in distributed vector storage systems. Leading developments include:

**HARMONY** demonstrates 4.63x throughput improvements over traditional distributed vector databases through its novel multi-granularity partition strategy[4]. The system combines dimension-based and vector-based partitioning with early-stop pruning mechanisms.

**HAKES** introduces a disaggregated architecture specifically designed for embedding search services[5]. The system achieves up to 16x higher throughput than baselines while maintaining high recall rates under concurrent read-write workloads.

**SOGAIC** addresses ultra-large-scale vector databases managing over 10 billion vectors with 47.3% reduction in construction time[6]. The system successfully deployed in industrial search engines serving hundreds of millions of users.

## Decentralized AI and Collective Intelligence

### Artificial Superintelligence Alliance

The **ASI Alliance** represents the world's largest open-source initiative for decentralized Artificial General Intelligence[7]. Formed in April 2024, it unites **SingularityNET**, **Fetch.ai**, **Ocean Protocol**, and **CUDOS** through a community-approved tokenomic merger[7].

Key achievements include:

- **ASI-1 Mini**: The world's first Web3-native large language model[8]
- **ASI Chain** launching in testnet with mainnet planned for Q3 2025[9]
- Unified \$FET token ecosystem supporting over 300 experts[7]
- Modular infrastructure combining scalable compute, data, and identity layers[7]
